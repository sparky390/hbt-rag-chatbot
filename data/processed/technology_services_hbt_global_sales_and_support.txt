## HBT Technology Services
HBT combines deep engineering expertise, industrial automation excellence, and data-driven innovation to help organizations build smarter, more efficient, and future-ready operations. We deliver end-to-end capabilities—from product engineering and embedded software to automation, data services, and lifecycle support—under one trusted partner.

### HBT Engineering
HBT [Hauhinco Bergbau Technik] subsidiaries and partner offices are strategically located in 15 countries including Australia, United States, China, Germany, India, Russia and headquartered in Luenen, Germany. It is a world class machine manufacturer and fully integrated single solution provider – specializing in systems and equipment for underground coal mining, Tunnelling, High Pressure Pumps, Control Systems, Drives & Motors.
HBT Engineering Private Limited, India is a company consisting of three primary departments that play a crucial role in supporting the Global HBT Group Business Technology services. These departments include Automation Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Global Product Support.
